Somehow, naked mole rats got even weirder in 2017. It turns out the cold-blooded, cancer-resistant, pain-immune and conspicuously long-lived rodents also can survive for extended periods of time without oxygen. In tests at the University of Illinois, biologists (carefully) deprived naked mole rats of oxygen for 18 minutes; in a separate test, the researchers kept them for more than five hours at dangerously low oxygen levels.
